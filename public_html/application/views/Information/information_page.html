FAQ

I received a speeding ticket. What's next?

Please pay requested amount on the citation document.

I lost my citation documents. How can I look up information relevant to my speeding ticket?

Please use the search box on our website.

I received a court date. What must I do?

Please appear in court at requested time, or contact court for extension.

What should I do before I go to court?

Contact a lawyer who can represent your rights.

What happens in court?

A judge will hear your case and will make judgement according to all available information.

What are my rights?

You have the right to get legal representation in court.

How can I get a lawyer?

Please work with the state court to get recommendations, or visit the website of the Missouri BAR Association.

What will happen if I ignore the ticket?

Your case will be brought to the court system and will be tried by an assigned judge.
